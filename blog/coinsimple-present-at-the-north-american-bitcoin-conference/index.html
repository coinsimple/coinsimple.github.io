CoinSimple Present at the North American Bitcoin Conference

Updated on January 2, 2015

Hong Kong, January 2, 2015: Two entrepreneurs from Seedcoin’s first group of startups have been invited to participate at one of the largest Bitcoin conferences in the US - The North American Bitcoin Conference, set for January 16-18, 2015, at the Fillmore Theatre in Miami Beach, Florida. CoinSimple Founder Nikos Bentenitis and meXBT CEO and Founder Gabriel Miron will light up the conference startup stage. These two pioneers are backed by Hong Kong-based Bitcoin startup virtual incubator Seedcoin.

Hong Kong based e-commerce platform CoinSimple and Latin America’s leading Bitcoin exchange meXBT also will exhibit at this major event. meXBT is committed to grant easy and broad access to crypto currencies in Latin America, and as such the Latin community in Florida can also benefit from meXBT’s professional trading platform. meXBT has recently partnered with innovative industry leaders enabling the company to provide for the unbanked population, especially in Mexico as it’s the world’s third largest corridor for remittance transactions. meXBT will make another important announcement at The North American Bitcoin Conference, furthering the company’s ability to tackle the remittance market. “We are honored to be invited to present at the North American Bitcoin Conference in Miami, which is becoming the hub for Fintech Latin American firms, and meXBT is driving adoption for new ways to transfer value across borders in the region,” said Miron.

CoinSimple has developed e-commerce plug-ins and online tools that make it extremely simple for e-commerce merchants and professionals to accept Bitcoin or other cryptocurrencies: decentralized, hassle-free and developer-free. CoinSimple made the headlines earlier this year when it launched its Bitcoin payment solutions with the support of CoinSimple advisor Andreas Antonopoulos, cryptocurrency advocate and author of Mastering Bitcoin and CoinDesk’s most influential person in Bitcoin in 2014.
