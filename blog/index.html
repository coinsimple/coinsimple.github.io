CoinSimple and MeXBT working to integrate their platforms

Updated on January 17, 2015.

Miami, January 17, 2015: Hong Kong-based e-commerce platform CoinSimple and meXBT, one of Latin America’s leading Bitcoin exchanges will integrate to give their customers the best of both worlds. On behalf of the two Seedcoin-backed startups, CoinSimple Founder and CEO Nikos Bentenitis and meXBT Founder and CEO Gabriel Miron announced the news today during The North American Bitcoin Conference.

CoinSimple Present at the North American Bitcoin Conference

Updated on January 2, 2015.

Hong Kong, January 2, 2015: Two entrepreneurs from Seedcoin’s first group of startups have been invited to participate at one of the largest Bitcoin conferences in the US - The North American Bitcoin Conference, set for January 16-18, 2015, at the Fillmore Theatre in Miami Beach, Florida. CoinSimple Founder Nikos Bentenitis and meXBT CEO and Founder Gabriel Miron will light up the conference startup stage. These two pioneers are backed by Hong Kong-based Bitcoin startup virtual incubator Seedcoin.

Bitcoin expert Andreas M. Antonopoulos joins CoinSimple

Updated on September 15, 2014.

Hong Kong, September 15, 2014: CoinSimple, the service that makes it extremely easy for consultants and e-commerce merchants to accept bitcoin, is proud to announce that Bitcoin expert Andreas M. Antonopoulos has joined the company as an Advisor to the Board. Andreas has been involved in various roles with CoinSimple since the company’s formation and his role as an advisor will allow him to help shape CoinSimple’s future and core product.
