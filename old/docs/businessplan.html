### Monero - Stripe for Bitcoin ## Products and Services The Bitcoin payment industry is growing fast with companies like BitPay, Coinbase and BIPS signing up an increasing number of merchants. (BitPay, for example, grew its merchant base from 1,000 to 10,000 in one year, as mentioned in [this article](http://techcrunch.com/2013/09/16/bitpay-10000-merchants/)) In addition, new payment processors, like BitPagos, GoCoin and Circle [(see this article)](http://www.coindesk.com/bitcoin-jeremy-allaire-circle/) are entering the market. These developments create two market needs. *First*, merchants need help with selecting payment processors and with integrating the infrastructure of the payment processors into their systems. To address this market need, Monero will offer the following product and service. 1. Bitcoin-to-fiat currency payment plugins that make it easy for merchants to integrate their favorite Bitcoin payment processor (BitPay, Coinbase, BIPS, or others). Plugins will be developed for Wordpress, Drupal and 21 more platforms. Merchants can easily switch from one payment processor to another. 2. Software as a Service, SaaS, for merchants that would like additional features compared to the plugins, for instance, price optimizations. A complementary mobile application that runs using the private API of the SaaS website for point of sale (POS) or person-to-person transactions. *Second*, payment processors need to sign more merchants and compete more effectively. To address this market need, Monero will create alliances with the payment processors (BitPay, CoinBase, BIPS, Circle), bring customers to them and ask for referral fees. ## Target market The Bitcoin-to-fiat merchant processing market is expanding rapidly and companies around the world can now accept payments. Monero’s focus will be on both existing markets where merchants are looking for options and ease-of-use as well as untapped markets, like Asia. Our customers are early-adopter companies and marketing to them should happen through specialized forums. Their motivations are to move fast to market and to have a reliable system for accepting Bitcoin. Our potential partners are merchant processing companies like BitPay, Coinbase, BIPS and Circle and they are interested in the customers that we will bring them. Partnerships with hosting companies like WPEngine and e-commerce companies like BigCommerce should also be considered. ## Competition Established payment processing companies (Authorize.net for instance) might enter the Bitcoin market. Our approach is to move fast so that Monero could be a strong competitor or subject to acquisition.** **Being first to market gives us an advantage, and forming a strong community of developers that support us will be extremely valuable. Although the barrier to entry for future competitors is currently not that large, our experience in the space will make it larger as time goes on. ## Marketing and Sales Our marketing messages for our plugin and SaaS customers will be "We put you in control", and “Stripe for Bitcoin.” For our strategic partnerships, it will be “We deliver great work on time”. To market our products we will use trade shows, speaking engagements, networking, social media, licensing agreements and strategic partnerships For sales, we will outsource lead generation. We will establish an affiliate program and affiliates will get compensated for every referral. ## Financial projections A detailed outline of the financial projections for one year after the launch of the SaaS product is given [in this spreadsheet](http://bit.ly/1cuewJf).
