How can I accept bitcoin directly to my Armory wallet using CoinSimple?

Updated on January 13, 2015.

In order to use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to your Armory bitcoin wallet, you need to export your watching-only wallet file and the associated first receiving address from Armory. The first receiving address will be used by CoinSimple to verify that you have entered consistent information. This guide explains how to do that for version 0.92.3 of the Armory wallet.

If you experience problems with these instructions, please upgrade your wallet by visiting this link.

Follow these steps:

  1. Start Armory, click on your preferred wallet from “Available Wallets” and lick on the “Wallet Properties” button on the left.
  2. A new “Wallet Properties” window will appear. Click on the “Export Watching-Only Copy” button on the right of the screen.
Armory Wallet Properties

Click on the “Export Watching-Only Copy” button on the right of the screen.

  1. In the “Watch-Only Wallet Export” window that appears, click on the “Export Watching-Only Wallet File” in the middle of the window.
Armory Wallet Properties

Click on the “Export Watching-Only Wallet File” in the middle of the window.

  1. Save file with the “.watchonly.wallet” extension on your Desktop. Press “Done” in the “Watch-Only Wallet Export” window.
  2. Go to the CoinSimple website, navigate to “Settings”, “Wallets and Processors”, and click on “Armory”.
  3. In the form that appears, press the “Choose File” button and upload the “.watchonly.wallet” file.
  4. Return to Armory. In the “Addresses in Wallet” section of the “Wallet Properties” window, you will see a list of addresses. Right-click on the first address, and press “Copy to Clipboard”.
Armory Wallet Addresses

Right-click on the first address, and press “Copy to Clipboard”.

  1. Return to the Armory page on the CoinSimple website. Paste the copied the key in the “For verification, paste the first address of your wallet” field.
  2. Click “Submit”
  3. You will need to verify the addition of this Armory wallet. So, check your email for a message from CoinSimple and click on the relevant link.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly with your Armory deterministic wallet.
