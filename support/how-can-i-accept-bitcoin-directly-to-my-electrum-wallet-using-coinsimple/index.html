How can I accept bitcoin directly to my Electrum wallet using CoinSimple?

Updated on January 13, 2015.

In order to use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to your Electrum bitcoin wallet, you need to extract your master public key and first receiving address from Electrum. The first receiving address will be used by CoinSimple to verify that you have entered consistent information. This guide explains how to do that for version 1.9.8 of the Electrum wallet.

If you experience problems with these instructions, please upgrade your wallet by visiting this link.

Follow these steps:

  1. Start Electrum and click on the “Wallet” menu and then on “Master Public Key”.
Electrum Menu: Master Public Key

Click on the “Wallet” menu and then on “Master Public Key”

  1. A “Master Public Key” window will appear. Copy the long series of letters and numbers shown on the left and press “Close”.
Your Electrum master public key

Copy the long series of letters and numbers shown on the left and press “Close”

  1. Go to the CoinSimple website, navigate to “Settings”, “Wallets and Processors”, and click on “Electrum”. In the form that appears, paste the copied the key in the “Enter your Electrum master public key” field.
  2. Return to Electrum and click on the “Receive” tab.
  3. In the “Receiving” section of the window that opens, you will see a list of addresses. Right-click on the first address, and press “Copy to Clipboard”.
Your first Electrum receiving address

Right-click on the first address, and press “Copy to Clipboard”

  1. Return to the Electrum page on the CoinSimple website. Paste the copied the key in the “For verification, paste the first address of your wallet” field.
  2. Click “Submit”
  3. You will need to verify the addition of this Electrum wallet. So, check your email for a message from CoinSimple and click on the relevant link.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly with your Electrum deterministic wallet.
