How can I accept bitcoin directly to my Electrum wallet using CoinSimple?

In order to use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to your Electrum bitcoin wallet, you need to extract your master public key and first receiving address from Electrum. The first receiving address will be used by CoinSimple to verify that you have entered consistent information. This guide explains how to do that for version 1.9.8 of the Electrum wallet.

How can I accept bitcoin directly to my Armory wallet using CoinSimple?

In order to use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to your Armory bitcoin wallet, you need to export your watching-only wallet file and the associated first receiving address from Armory. The first receiving address will be used by CoinSimple to verify that you have entered consistent information. This guide explains how to do that for version 0.92.3 of the Armory wallet.

How can I accept bitcoin to my Trezor wallet with CoinSimple?

In order to use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to your Trezor hardware wallet, you need to find your master public key of your Trezor device. The first receiving address will be used by CoinSimple to verify that you have entered consistent information.
