How can I use CoinSimple with Wordpress eCommerce?

The CoinSimple WP e-Commerce plugin is an extension of the WP e-Commerce plugin for Wordpress, and allows e-commerce stores, running on Wordpress and WP e-Commerce, to accept bitcoin through CoinSimple.

How can I use CoinSimple with my Website or Email Newsletter?

This guide explains how to create a button or link on your website, or on your email newsletter, so that you can sell your products and services using CoinSimple.
