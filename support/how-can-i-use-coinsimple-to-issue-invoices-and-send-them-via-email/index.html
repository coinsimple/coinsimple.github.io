How can I use CoinSimple to issue invoices and send them via email?

This guide explains how to issue your first CoinSimple invoice, in 2 steps. The process is quite simple but the steps are described here in great detail.

If you haven’t already done so, set up an account with CoinSimple. Detailed instructions on how to do that, can be found here.

1. Create an invoice

All you have to do is click on “Invoices” and then on “New Invoice”. At that point you need to enter or choose:

  • You client’s name
  • Your client’s email
  • The currency that you want to price your invoice in (all national currencies are supported)
  • The wallet or payment processor that you want to receive your funds to
  • The source of bitcoin price (Bitcoin Average, CoinDesk, Blockchain.info, Bitstamp, BitPay, Coinbase, GoCoin) or a set price
  • The name of your invoice items, their numbers and their unit price

You can also, optionally, choose a discount or to make your invoice recurring, on the same day of every month, or every a certain number of days. Recurring invoices will be sent perpetually, or for a certain number of times.

2. Save the invoice and select delivery options

At this point, you can send your invoice via email, review it, edit it, or delete it.
