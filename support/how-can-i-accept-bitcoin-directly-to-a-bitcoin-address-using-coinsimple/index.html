How can I accept bitcoin directly to a bitcoin address using CoinSimple?

Updated on January 13, 2015.

You can use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to any bitcoin address. This guide shows you how to find your bitcoin address in several wallets.

Example Wallets

  • Bitcoin Core
  • MultiBit
  • Hive
  • Green Address
  • Blockchain.info

Bitcoin Core

  1. Start Bitcoin Core and click on the “File” menu and the on “Receiving Addresses”.
  2. Select the address that you would like to use and click on the “Copy” button on the lower left.
  3. Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the bitcoin address.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly to your Bitcoin Core wallet.

MultiBit

  1. Start MultiBit and click on the “Trade” menu and the on “Request Bitcoin”.
  2. On the right panel, you will see a Bitcoin address. Press on the button that is right next to the address to copy it to your clipboard.
  3. Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the bitcoin address.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly to your MultiBit wallet.

Back To Top

Hive

  1. Start Hive and hover your mouse over the Bitcoin address of your choice.
  2. Click on the address and it will be copied to your clipboard.
  3. Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the bitcoin address.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly to your Hive wallet.

Back To Top

Green Address

  1. Start Green Address and click on “Receive Money” link on the left.
  2. Click on “Show URI and address”.
  3. Select the bitcoin address that starts with either 1 or 3 and copy it.
  4. Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the bitcoin address.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly to your Green Address wallet.

Back To Top

Blockchain.info

  1. Login to Blockchain.info.
  2. On the lower left of the screen, you will see a box that says “This Is Your Bitcoin Address”. Copy the address to your clipboard.
  3. Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the bitcoin address.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly to your Blockchain.info wallet.
