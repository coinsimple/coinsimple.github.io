How can I set up an account with CoinSimple?

This guide will explain the process for signing up with CoinSimple in 3 steps. The process is quite simple but the steps are described in great detail.

1. Sign Up for a CoinSimple account

Go to the CoinSimple Sign Up page. Enter your name, your email and select a password. After you read the CoinSimple Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, and check that you agree with them.

2. Set up a business

The app will automatically lead you to a page to set up a new business. (In order to create an invoice, you need to first create a business, that is why, the app will not let you move to any other part of the site, before you set up a business.) In the form that appears, enter the name of your business and its description. Both the name and description of your business appear in the invoices that you send to your customers, so choose them appropriately. Also, enter the currency that you use most commonly for your invoices (you can change that later and even for every invoice that you issue). Finally, enter the source of the bitcoin price that you will most commonly use for your invoices (again, you can change the source later, and for every invoice, if you like.) After you select whether you want to receive emails when you create a new invoice and when you get paid, you are done.

3. Connect your wallet or payment processor

The app will now lead you to a page to connect your wallet or your existing bitcoin processor account. (In order to get paid for your invoices, you will need to have a wallet where the funds will be directed – CoinSimple does not offer a wallet service. That is why, the app will not let you move to any other part of the site, if you don’t set up wallet.) These support pages explain how you can connect your Electrum, Armory, Trezor, or any bitcoin address. At this point you can add additional wallets and bitcoin payment processors to your business to give yourself more flexibility.
