How can I accept bitcoin to my Trezor wallet with CoinSimple?

In order to use CoinSimple to accept bitcoins directly to your Trezor hardware wallet, you need to find your master public key of your Trezor device. The first receiving address will be used by CoinSimple to verify that you have entered consistent information.

Step 1: Connect your Trezor hardware device to your computer and navigate to mytrezor.com. On the main screen, click on “Add Account” on the left.

MyTrezor.com Account Creation

Click on "Add Account" on the left

Step 2: “Account 3” is created by the software and it can be seen on the left as in the image below. In this demo, we will use “Account 3” for payment processing. Click on the “Advanced details” button.

MyTrezor.com Main Screen

Click on the "Advanced details" button.

Step 3: Find “Account 3” under the “Account public keys (XPUB)” section, and copy the long alphanumeric code that starts with “xpub” for “Account 3”.

Trezor account master public key

Copy the long alphanumeric code that starts with "xpub"

Step 4: Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the xpub key in the “Enter your account public key” field.

Step 5: Return to the MyTrezor.com website and click on your Account on the left of the screen. Click on the “Receive” tab and copy the address that is shown that starts with “1”.

Trezor first receiving address

Click on the "Export Watching-Only Copy" button on the right of the screen.

Step 6: Return to the CoinSimple website and paste the key in the “Enter the first address” field.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly with your Trezor hierarchical deterministic wallet.
