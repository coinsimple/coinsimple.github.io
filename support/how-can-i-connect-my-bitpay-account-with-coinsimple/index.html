How can I connect my BitPay account with CoinSimple?

In order to use CoinSimple to integrate with the BitPay bitcoin payment processor, you need to pair your BitPay account with CoinSimple. This guide explains how to do that.

Please follow these steps:

Step 1: Login to your BitPay account and click on the menu link in the upper left part of your screen.

Step 2: Click on ‘Settings’.

BitPay Menu: Settings

Login to your BitPay account and click on the menu link in the upper left part of your screen. Click on 'Settings'.

Step 3: Scroll down the menu until you find the ‘Developer Resources’ subsection of the Settings menu. Click on ‘API Tokens’.

BitPay Menu: API Tokens

Scroll down the menu until you find the 'Developer Resources' subsection of the Settings menu. Click on 'API Tokens'.

Step 4: Click on ‘Add New Token’.

BitPay Menu: Add New Token

Click on 'Add New Token'.

Step 5: In the ‘Token Label’ field enter a name for your token, for example ‘CoinSimple’ and press ‘Add Token’.

BitPay Form: Add Token

In the 'Token Label' field enter a name for your token, for example 'CoinSimple' and press 'Add Token'.

Step 6: Copy the ‘Pairing Code’ shown and copy it.

BitPay Pairing Code

Copy the 'Pairing Code' shown and copy it.

Step 7: Go to the CoinSimple website, navigate to “Settings”, “Wallets and Processors”, and click on “BitPay”. In the form that appears, paste the copied pairing code.

Step 8: Click “Submit”

Step 9: You will need to verify the addition of your BitPay account. So, check your email for a message from CoinSimple and click on the relevant link.

Congratulations! You can now start receiving bitcoin directly to the BitPay account.
