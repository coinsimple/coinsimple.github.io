How can I connect my GoCoin account with CoinSimple?

Updated on January 20, 2015.

In order to connect your GoCoin account with CoinSimple, you need to allow the CoinSimple application that we have set up at GoCoin.com to create invoices on your behalf. The process is interactive and it starts as soon as you press the “Connect” button for the GoCoin payment processor on the CoinSimple settings page.
